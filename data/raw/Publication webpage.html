Detail

This statistical first release (SFR) provides updated information on the overall achievements of young people in GCSE examinations and other regulated qualifications in 2012 to 2013. The data has been collated for the 2013 secondary school performance tables, which has been checked by schools.

The SFR explores 3 areas of interest within the data:

  • school floor standard
  • pupils achieving A* grades in GCSE/iGCSE examinations
  • attainment of pupils by prior attainment band
  • the unusual English attainment of pupils in independent schools
